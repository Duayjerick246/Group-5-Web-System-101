Bongbong Marcos

Background: Born on September 13, 1957, in Manila, Bongbong Marcos is the son of former President Ferdinand Marcos Sr.

Biography: He took office as President on June 30, 2022, after winning the 2022 presidential election.

Contributions:

  • Economic Development: Focused on reviving the economy post-COVID-19 pandemic.
  • Infrastructure Projects: Continued the "Build, Build, Build" program initiated by previous administrations.
  • Food Security: Launched initiatives aimed at improving agricultural productivity and addressing food shortages.

Highlights: Economic Revitalization, Infrastructure Development, Food Security Initiatives
