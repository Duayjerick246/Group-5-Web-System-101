Joseph Estrada

Background: Born on April 19, 1937, in Manila, Joseph Estrada was a popular actor before entering politics.

Biography: Serving as president from 1998 until his ousting in 2001, Estrada's presidency was marked by controversies and allegations of corruption.

Contributions:

  • Social Programs: Launched several initiatives aimed at poverty alleviation and housing.
  • Infrastructure Development: Invested in various infrastructure projects to improve transportation.
  • Entertainment Industry: Promoted the local film and entertainment industry during his term.

Highlights: Former Actor, Advocate for Social Welfare, Controversial Presidency

Gloria Macapagal Arroyo

Background: Born on April 5, 1947, in Lubao, Pampanga, Arroyo is the daughter of former President Diosdado Macapagal.

Biography: She served as president from 2001 to 2010, focusing on economic reforms and development. Arroyo faced numerous challenges, including allegations of election fraud.

Contributions:

  • Economic Growth: Implemented policies that led to economic stability and growth during her administration.
  • Education Reforms: Focused on improving the education system and access to quality education.
  • Health Programs: Expanded health care programs aimed at improving public health services.

Highlights: First Female President for Two Terms, Economic Reformer, Education Advocate
