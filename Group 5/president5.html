Diosdado Macapagal

Background: Diosdado Macapagal was born on September 28, 1914, in Lubao, Pampanga. He was a lawyer and politician, known for his advocacy of social justice.

Biography: Macapagal served as the ninth President of the Philippines from 1961 to 1965. He focused on land reform and anti-poverty programs.

Contributions:

  • Land Reform: Implemented land redistribution to benefit farmers.
  • Anti-Poverty: Launched programs aimed at poverty alleviation.
  • Foreign Relations: Improved ties with the United States and other nations.

Highlights: Advocate for Social Justice, Focus on Economic Reforms

Ferdinand Marcos

Background: Ferdinand Marcos was born on September 11, 1917, in Sarrat, Ilocos Norte. He served as a lawyer before entering politics.

Biography: Marcos served as the tenth President of the Philippines from 1965 until 1986. His administration was marked by martial law and significant infrastructure development.

Contributions:

  • Martial Law: Declared martial law in 1972 to combat insurgency and dissent.
  • Infrastructure: Initiated major infrastructure projects across the country.
  • Economic Policies: Promoted industrialization and foreign investment.

Highlights: Controversial Martial Law Era, Infrastructure Development
