My Early Childhood

Jerick Duay was born on March 20, 2006, in the quiet town of E.B. Magalona, Negros Occidental. He spent his formative years there, attending E.B. Magalona Elementary School from kindergarten up to grade 4. His childhood was filled with memories of life in the province, where he built strong friendships and developed a love for learning. However, in grade 5, his life took a significant turn when he and his family migrated to Manila. Adjusting to life in the bustling city, Jerick continued his education at San Agustin Elementary School, where he completed grades 5 and 6. These experiences taught him resilience and adaptability, as he transitioned from his rural hometown to the fast-paced urban life of Manila. After finishing elementary school, he enrolled in Novaliches High School, where he thrived academically and socially. In senior high school, he chose the STEM strand, focusing on Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics, which helped him lay a solid foundation for his future in the IT field. He graduated from Novaliches High School with a sense of accomplishment, ready to take on the challenges of higher education.

My Currently Life

Currently, Jerick is pursuing a degree in Information Technology at Quezon City University, a field he is passionate about. His interest in IT started early and has grown stronger over time, especially with his work immersion experience at Colegio de St. Teresa de Avila. During his work immersion, he taught students how IT works, sharing his knowledge and skills in a hands-on environment, which gave him practical insight into the real-world applications of IT. Apart from his academic pursuits, Jerick has a variety of hobbies that keep him active and engaged. He loves playing basketball, both in physical games and on mobile platforms, balancing his physical activity with his love for technology. He is also into mobile gaming, a hobby that ties closely to his interest in coding and technology. As he progresses through his IT course, he continues to enhance his skills in programming, excited about the opportunities that his growing knowledge will bring. Jerick is also a fan of the P-pop group BINI, and he particularly admires members like BINI Maloi and Mikha, among others, finding them cute and inspiring. For him, following BINI brings joy and entertainment, providing a fun break from his academic life.
