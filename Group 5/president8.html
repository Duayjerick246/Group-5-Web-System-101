Benigno Aquino III

Background: Born on February 8, 1960, in Manila, Aquino was the son of former President Corazon Aquino.

Biography: He served as president from 2010 to 2016, focusing on anti-corruption and good governance.

Contributions:

  • Philippine Economic Growth: His administration saw significant economic growth and improvements in investor confidence.
  • Good Governance: Promoted transparency and accountability in government through various reforms.
  • Social Programs: Expanded access to education and healthcare, benefiting millions of Filipinos.

Highlights: Anti-Corruption Advocate, Economic Reformer, Advocate for Good Governance

Rodrigo Duterte

Background: Born on March 28, 1945, in Maasin, Southern Leyte, Duterte served as the mayor of Davao City for over two decades.

Biography: Serving from 2016 to 2022, Duterte's administration focused on crime reduction and infrastructure development.

Contributions:

  • War on Drugs: Launched a controversial anti-drug campaign aimed at reducing crime rates.
  • Build, Build, Build Program: Initiated massive infrastructure projects to improve transportation and connectivity.
  • Foreign Policy Shift: Realigned the Philippines' foreign policy, fostering closer ties with China.

Highlights: Crime Reduction Focus, Infrastructure Development, Controversial Policies
